Become a Contributor
365Connect Community
Welcome to the 365Connect Community! We're excited that you want to become a contributor and help us promote and advance Microsoft D365 and Power Platform technologies. There are several ways you can contribute to our community:

1. Submit a Community Blog: (Beta)

If you have valuable insights, tips, or knowledge related to D365 and Power Platform, you can contribute by writing a community blog. Here's how:

  1. Fill out the Community Blog Submission Form with your blog post details.
  2. Our team will review your submission, and once approved, it will be published on our Community Blog.

2. Submit a Sample Solution:

Contribute to our GitHub repository with sample solutions or improvements.

  1. View Detailed Steps here: How to Submit a Sample.

3. Share Content and Events: (Coming Soon)

Help us by sharing valuable content and information about events related to D365 and Power Platform. Here's how:

  1. Share relevant content, articles, or resources on your social media profiles or within our community.
  2. Announce and promote D365 and Power Platform events, webinars, or meetups.

4. Speak at an Event:

If you have expertise in these technologies, consider becoming a speaker at one of our community events. Here's how:

  1. Submit your topic proposal and speaker details to our event organizers.
  2. If selected, prepare and deliver your presentation to our community.

5. Update Our Website: (Beta)

If you notice any bugs or have suggestions for improving our website, you can contribute by forking our repository and submitting a pull request with your changes. Here's how:

  1. Fork our website's GitHub repository.
  2. Create a new branch for your changes.
  3. Make the necessary updates or fixes.
  4. Submit a pull request to merge your changes into our website.

Together, we can make the 365Connect Community a vibrant hub for D365 and Power Platform enthusiasts. Get started today and help us grow!
